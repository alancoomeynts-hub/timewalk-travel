Seville, Ronda, Granada & Gibraltar

Journey through Andalusia's golden triangle of Moorish architecture, cliffside pueblos blancos, and flamenco soul. From Seville's gilded Alcázar where Columbus planned voyages, to Granada's Alhambra glowing at sunset, Ronda's impossible gorge bridge, and Gibraltar's apes guarding the Mediterranean gateway. This is Spain's most passionate, romantic region.

Andalusia begins in Seville — Europe's most romantic city. Your guide reveals the Moorish-Christian artistry of the Alcázar, the world's largest Gothic cathedral where Columbus rests, and Plaza de España's tiled glory. Evenings immerse you in authentic flamenco born in Triana's gypsy caves.

  • Private tour Alcázar of Seville — patios, gardens, Game of Thrones locations
  • Visit Seville Cathedral & Giralda Tower — Columbus tomb, panoramic views
  • Explore Plaza de España — tiled bridges, rowboats, neo-Moorish masterpiece
  • Private flamenco show — Triana tablaos with gypsy artists

Private transfer northwest reveals Ronda — Spain's most dramatic town straddling El Tajo gorge. Then wander Andalusia's iconic white villages clinging to cliffs amid olive groves.

  • Guided tour Ronda's Puente Nuevo — 120m gorge views, oldest bullring
  • Explore white villages — Setenil de las Bodegas (cave houses), Zahara de la Sierra
  • Visit Grazalema Natural Park — griffon vultures, mountain scenery

South to Europe's edge — Gibraltar's limestone monolith guarding the Mediterranean. Cable car to the summit reveals Africa across the strait.

  • Cable car to Rock summit — 400m panoramic views to Morocco
  • Meet the Barbary macaques — Europe's only wild monkeys
  • Explore WWII tunnels & siege galleries
  • Return to Ronda region

The architectural climax — Granada's Nasrid Alhambra glowing atop its hill. Gypsy caves echo with flamenco in the hillside Albaicín.

  • Alhambra sunset tickets — Nasrid Palaces, Generalife gardens
  • Private tour Albaicín quarter — miradors, Moorish streets
  • Flamenco cave concert — Sacromonte gypsy caves
  • Day trip Córdoba Mezquita — 856 columns, mosque-cathedral

Farewell transfer to Málaga Airport — gateway to Andalusia's coast.

  • Private transfer Granada → Málaga Airport (1h30m)
  • Optional beach time in Málaga
