Your guided holiday to Rome, Naples and the Amalfi Coast

Step back through 2,000 years of history on this immersive guided tour through Italy's most storied cities. From the ancient forums of Imperial Rome to the buried streets of Pompeii, the subterranean secrets of Naples and the dramatic clifftop villages of the Amalfi Coast, this journey combines expert-led cultural exploration with the freedom to discover Italy at your own pace.

Your journey begins in Rome, where 2,000 years of history line every street. Over three days your expert historian guide will lead you through the ancient heart of the city — from the gladiatorial arena of the Colosseum to the sweeping ruins of the Roman Forum and the imperial grandeur of Palatine Hill. Wander the cobbled lanes of Trastevere, cross the ancient Ponte Sisto and explore the Pantheon — the best-preserved building of the ancient world. Evenings are yours to discover Rome's legendary piazzas, trattorias and rooftop bars.

  • Guided tour of the Colosseum, Roman Forum & Palatine Hill
  • Visit the Pantheon and Piazza della Rotonda
  • Explore Imperial Rome — Trajan's Column, the Vittoriano and Capitoline Hill
  • Free evening time in Trastevere and Campo de' Fiori
  • Optional: Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel (pre-bookable)

Transfer south to Naples — Italy's most raw, electric and historically rich city. Beneath its chaotic streets lies a subterranean world of Greek tunnels, Roman aqueducts and wartime shelters. Above ground, the legendary Spaccanapoli cuts through the city's baroque heart. A full day excursion takes you to Pompeii — the city frozen in time by the catastrophic eruption of 79AD — followed by a hike to the crater rim of Mount Vesuvius for panoramic views over the Bay of Naples. The National Archaeological Museum houses the world's finest collection of Pompeii artefacts, including the haunting plaster casts of the eruption's victims.

  • Guided tour of Naples' Underground — ancient Greek and Roman tunnels
  • Walk the Spaccanapoli — street food, baroque churches, daily Neapolitan life
  • Full day at Pompeii — excavated streets, villas, forums and baths
  • Hike to the crater of Mount Vesuvius — sweeping views over the Bay of Naples
  • Visit the National Archaeological Museum — finest Roman collection in the world
  • Optional: Royal Palace of Caserta — Italy's answer to Versailles

Your final stage is based in the clifftop town of Sorrento — the perfect gateway to the Amalfi Coast. Wind along the dramatic coastal road past lemon groves, whitewashed villages and turquoise bays. One full free day in Amalfi gives you the freedom to explore the stunning Cathedral of Sant'Andrea, swim in the clear waters beneath the cliffs, browse the ceramic shops of Atrani or hop on a ferry to the glamorous island of Capri or the pastel-coloured village of Positano. It's the perfect balance of guided exploration and Italian freedom.

  • Stay in Sorrento — scenic clifftop base above the Bay of Naples
  • Guided coastal drive through Positano, Praiano and Ravello
  • Visit Ravello's Villa Rufolo gardens — sweeping Amalfi Coast panoramas
  • Free day in Amalfi — explore, swim, shop or ferry to Positano or Capri at leisure
  • Optional: Boat tour of the Amalfi coastline and sea caves
  • Farewell dinner in Sorrento before return transfer to Naples airport
